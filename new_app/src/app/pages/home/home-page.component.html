The top 1000 of medieval castles

In Europe and Asia more than 10,000 medieval castles can be found. Topcastles.com selected the most beautiful and interesting castles and ranked them in a top 1000 list. The construction of this list is explained in the menu background. You can add castles to your own collection in the menu favorites.
