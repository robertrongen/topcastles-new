The top 1000 of medieval castles

Across Europe and Asia, more than 10,000 medieval castles can still be found. Topcastles.com selected the most beautiful and interesting castles and ranked them in a top 1000 list. Every square picture of a castle on this site links to the information page of that castle. The methodology behind of this list is explained in the menu background.

Search, filter and sort the list and add castles to your favorites

The top 1000 list can be searched, filtered and sorted to show toplists per country or region or type of castle. You can add castles to your own collection in the menu favorites.

Top twelve by visitor rating

Between 2004 and 2021, 17,300 visitors of topcastles.com and topkastelen.nl* submitted 63,800 ratings for castles in the list. If you are interested in the most popular castles based on visitor ratings, you can click on the VISITORS* header in the table view of the top 1000 list.
